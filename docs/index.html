THE HUNT IS OVER

Robotsu is a friendly bot designed to keep you company and help you manage your Discord Guild. He comes with many interactive and fun commands to assist you express in a text chat what you would like to say in more than just words to other members, but cannot find the right words to say it.

Use feedback command to let his Devs know what other features interest you, or simply to send them encouragement notes and some love.

If you want a list of commands available simply type help command in a text channel or private message. As a primary focus Robotsu uses Kaomoji emotes over GIF or PNG images.

Most of his commands are presented in neat Embeds, and with very minimal use of images because the primary vision is that Robotsu shouldn't steal all the attention from the conversations.

Keep in mind that for the time being Robotsu is Free, so hurry and invite him into your Guild.

Soon he will also include a new surprise that's on the works.
