THE HUNT IS OVER

Robotsu is a friendly bot designed to keep you company and help you manage your Discord Guild. He comes with many interactive and fun commands to assist you express in a text chat what you would like to say in more than just words to other members, but cannot find the right words to say it.

Use feedback command to let his Devs know what other features interest you, or simply to send them encouragement and some love.

His Dev's primary focus was to have Robotsu use cute Kaomoji emotes instead of the overused GIF or PNG images that most bots use on Discord.

To see a list of commands available simply type help command in a text channel or private message. Most of his commands are presented in neat Embeds, and with very minimal use of images because the primary vision is that Robotsu shouldn't steal all the attention away from the conversations.
